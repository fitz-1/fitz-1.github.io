Meet the Creators

Claire Grossman

Head of Radical Ideas & Sketch-to-Stitch Magician

Growing up camping, skiing, and exploring the outdoors, Claire understands how the right gear can transform an adventure. With a background in product and apparel design, along with a love for hands-on problem solving, she helped bring the Nova Tent from early sketches to the final stitch. Her attention to detail, creative mindset, and sewing experience all came together to refine the tent's structure and user experience. For Claire, designing the Nova Tent was about more than just functionality, it was about making outdoor adventures easier, more comfortable, and a lot more fun.
